Deendayal Hastkala Sankul

Deendayal Hastkala Sankul is a cultural complex in Varanasi that promotes and preserves traditional crafts. It showcases the rich heritage of Indian handicrafts, providing a platform for artisans to display their skills.

Swarved Mahamandir

Swarved Mahamandir is a spiritual and cultural complex dedicated to the elevation of human consciousness. It serves as a center for spiritual teachings, meditation, and cultural activities.

Ramnagar Fort

Ramnagar Fort, situated on the banks of the Ganges, is a historic fortification that once served as the residence of the Maharaja of Varanasi. It reflects the architectural grandeur of a bygone era.

3D Man Mahal Museum

The 3D Man Mahal Museum offers a unique visual experience with its three-dimensional displays depicting the history and culture of Varanasi. It's a blend of art and technology.

Vishwanath Gali

Vishwanath Gali is a vibrant and bustling street in Varanasi, known for its vibrant markets, street food, and traditional artifacts. It provides a glimpse into the local culture and heritage.
